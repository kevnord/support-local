North Bend Restaurant Guide

Welcome!

Since many of the Snoqualmie Valley businesses have been severely affected by the COVID-19 outbreak including all our favorite restaurants, I created this website to try and help people find what restaurants are still open and what services they provide.

If our community finds this website is useful, I'll continue to add more restaurants throughout the valley including Snoqualmie and Fall City. If I've missed any restaurants or have any information incorrect please let me know and I will update it as soon as I can. You can contact me using the following Google Form: https://forms.gle/62vcce4ube6fc2BJA

I hope you find this useful. Let's help keep our local restaurants in business! Thanks for your help.
